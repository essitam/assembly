(work in progress)

September 22 2020*

(*Adapted from http://www.projectyu.ca/how-to-be-a-flaneur)

 

  1. Meet at the bus stop in front of the Spadina Steps on Davenport
  2. Take a left when you step off of the bus start
  3. When you pass a man wearing glasses turn immediately around and take your next left
  4. Look for a Tim Hortons coffee shop. Stop and observe your surroundings.
  5. Look for a direction that looks interesting and head that way
  6. Turn down the next alley that you come across and make a right when you come out of it.
  7. Look for a place to take a seat (bench, park, grass) and take a load off. Sit here for at least 10 minutes and see what unfolds.

September 29 2020

  1. Start walking towards a train track and walk at pacing steps of a waiting parent. When you get to the track wait to watch a train.
  2. Set a timer for 7 minutes. Walk on the nearest sidewalk parallel to the tracks. Every time you see a piece of bubble gum turn around and return in the direction you came from. Never turn around at the same piece of bubblegum twice. When the timer goes off, stop.
  3. Walk in the most boring direction until you find somewhere to sit.
  4. At the sitting spot, take a load off and see what unfolds, without talking, for 10 minutes.

 

October 6 2020

  1. Walk toward the nearest park. Have lots in the sun walking. Think about talking to trees. Look same tree until easy leaves fall.
  2. Walk in the direction you’re currently facing until you hit an obstacle. Turn left. Repeat seven times.
  3. Walk towards purple for 5 minutes.
  4. Go to the face of a building don’t move for good minute.
  5. Stand still for 3 minutes and listen. After the 3 minutes, walk in the direction of the sound that interests you most until you hit an obstacle. Stop there and listen for 1 more minute.
  6. Walk to a sit spot.

October 13 2020

  1. Ready the body with watching the trees sitting with enough time to really point the next instruction.
  2. Stand, and walk in a pleasing direction counting your paces up to one hundred.
  3. Walk to the nearest man-made patterned floor surface. Walk in a straight line, moving your arms in rhythm with the pattern of the floor texture. Continue until the floor pattern ends.
  4. Find a sit spot and sit for 10 minutes and see what unfolds.

October 20 2020

  1. Easy the mind and follow the good body towards a quiet place.
